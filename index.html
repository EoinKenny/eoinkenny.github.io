Home

I am an explainable AI reseracher currently doing my postdoc at MIT. Previously, I did my Ph.D. at University College Dublin, Ireland. My research vision is to design interpretable AI systems which reason in the same way humans do, so that we can communicate and with them and successfully utilize their abilities in sensitive domains such as medicine, finance, and autonomous vechicles.

Specifically, I believe in using exemplar/prototype theory, contrative explanation, and causal reasoning (either in the sense of the world or model) in this process.

Above all however, I am a strong advocate for testing these systems on real humans to prove their utility.

In a previous life, I was also a cello player!
