Home

I am an explainable AI reseracher currently doing my postdoc at MIT. Previously, I did my Ph.D. at University College Dublin, Ireland. Prior to this, I also completed my Bachelor of Music degree and Master of Arts in Musicology & Performance at the University of Maynooth.

My research vision is to design interpretable AI systems which reason in the same way humans do, so that we can clearly see what they are causally doing in a way that everyone can understand (not just ML experts). If this is realised, I believe we can more successfully utilize their abilities in sensitive domains such as medicine, finance, and autonomous vechicles.

Specifically, I believe in using exemplar/prototype theory, contrative explanation, and causal reasoning (either in the sense of the world or model) in this process.

Above all however, I am a strong advocate for testing these systems on real humans to prove their utility.

In a previous life, I was also a cello player!
